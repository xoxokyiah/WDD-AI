Welcome to the Cities Webpage

Here you will find information about various cities around the world.

New York

New York City is the largest city in the United States. It is known for its iconic landmarks such as the Statue of Liberty, Times Square, and Central Park.

Paris

Paris is the capital city of France. It is famous for its art, fashion, and culture, as well as landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital city of Japan. It is known for its modern architecture, shopping districts, and vibrant culture.
